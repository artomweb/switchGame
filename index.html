How to play:

  1. Enter your username and click submit
  2. Create a new tab or ask someone else to join the game
  3. Click the request button next to someone's username
  4. Ask the other player to accept your request
  5. The text underneath the game tells you who's turn it is

Rules:

  • The objective of the game is to make a square with pieces of your colour
  • Two of your pieces have the opponents colour on the botton, shown by being split down the middle
  • When it's your turn, you can either:
    1. Move one of your pieces onto the board
    2. Move one of your pieces around on the board
    3. Flip one of the oponents pieces over
